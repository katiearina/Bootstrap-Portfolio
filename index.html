About Me

Hello! My name is Katie (well, technically it's Katarina) and I live in Durham, North Carolina. I grew up on a farm in northern Maryland (only a half-mile from Pennsylvania!), spent nine years living in Portland, Oregon, and returned to the east coast in early 2014 to see what the South had to offer.

I have driven across the country five times, have two Oregon-born cats, and like to spend my free time eating spicy foods and fixing up my house (some photos can be found under the #cutehouseiscute tag on Instagram). Some of my favorite things to do in Durham are to go hiking on the Eno and explore new breweries.

I am a firm believer that sunshine can pretty much always cure what ails me, love to talk DIY and hot sauce preferences, and am interested in learning how to brew kombucha. One of my favorite new discoveries while learning web development is how many ipsum varieties exist out in the world; my two favorites so far are Hipster Ipsum and Cards Against Humanity Ipsum.

If you'd like to say hello (or if you have a SCOBY you'd like me to take off your hands!), please send me a message.
